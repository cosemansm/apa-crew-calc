04 Per-job override (UX detail)
🎯
Subtle engine selection at the job level
When creating or editing a job, a single dropdown reads "T&Cs: UK APA ▾" — pre-filled from the user's global default. For most users this is invisible. When needed, switch to "Belgian Deal Memo" and the entire calculator adapts: different role list, day types, currency, OT rules, mileage tiers. Existing saved days on other jobs remain untouched. No marketing, no splash — just there when you need it.
